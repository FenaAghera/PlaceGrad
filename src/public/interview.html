Interview Questions

Prepare with commonly asked questions in technical and HR rounds.

Technical Interview Questions

  • What is the difference between C and C++?
  • Explain the concept of OOP with real-life examples.
  • What is a REST API?
  • What are the differences between SQL and NoSQL?
  • What is a deadlock? How can it be avoided?
  • Explain normalization and denormalization in databases.
  • What is the difference between React and Angular?
  • How does the internet work?
  • Explain time and space complexity with examples.
  • What is the difference between GET and POST methods?

HR Interview Questions

  • Tell me about yourself.
  • What are your strengths and weaknesses?
  • Why should we hire you?
  • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  • Why do you want to work with us?
  • Tell me about a time you handled failure.
  • How do you handle stress and pressure?
  • Are you willing to relocate?
  • Describe a challenging project you worked on.
  • Do you have any questions for us?
