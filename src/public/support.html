Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

+

Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address, and you'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. Follow the link to create a new password.

How do I update my profile information?

+

Navigate to the Profile section after logging in. Click on "Edit Profile" to update your personal information, academic details, skills, and resume. Make sure to save your changes before exiting.

When are placement drives scheduled?

+

Placement drives are typically scheduled from January to April for final year students. Check the Companies section and Dashboard for real-time updates on upcoming drives and application deadlines.

What documents do I need for placements?

+

You'll need: Updated resume (PDF format), All academic transcripts, Identity proof (Aadhar/Passport), Recent passport-size photographs, and any relevant certificates or portfolios.

How can I prepare for technical interviews?

+

Access our Training Materials section for practice questions, mock interviews, and technical resources. Attend workshops regularly and consider one-on-one sessions with our career counselors.

Can I apply to multiple companies?

+

Yes, you can apply to multiple companies unless there are specific restrictions mentioned in the job posting. However, if you receive an offer, you must inform the placement office immediately.
